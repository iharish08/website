At Bonelli's Gym, we're dedicated to helping you lead a healthier, more active lifestyle. Our mission is to create a welcoming and motivating environment where individuals of all fitness levels can achieve their goals and transform their lives.

Our Story

Founded in 2004, Bonelli's Gym began with a simple vision: to provide a community-focused fitness experience that goes beyond just working out. What started as a small local gym has grown into a thriving fitness center known for its exceptional facilities and passionate team.

Our Facilities

Our gym boasts cutting-edge equipment and a variety of spaces designed to meet all your fitness needs:

  • Cardio Zone: Featuring the latest treadmills, ellipticals, and bikes.
  • Strength Training Area: Equipped with free weights, resistance machines, and functional training tools.
  • Group Fitness Studios: Offering a range of classes from yoga and Pilates to high-intensity interval training (HIIT).
  • Relaxation Area: Unwind and recover with our sauna and steam room.
